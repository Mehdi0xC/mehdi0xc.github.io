About

I'm a second-year master's student in Computer Science at Simon Fraser University (SFU) and a member of the GrUVi (Graphics+Vision) lab. My supervisor is Prof. Ali Mahdavi-Amiri, and I'm doing research in 3D Representation Learning and Generative (Diffusion) Models.

I got my bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic), where I focused on Reinforcement Learning and Sensorimotor Systems.
